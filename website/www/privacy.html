Controller and contact

The controller for this website is the KoalaPull project maintainer, Timo (KoalaDev).

Privacy contact:

Website access

When you visit this static website, your browser necessarily sends technical request data such as your IP address, requested file, date and time, user agent, and referrer if supplied by your browser. We use this data only for delivering the website and maintaining security. We do not keep website logs.

Application data

KoalaPull does not send telemetry, analytics, download URLs, media metadata, history, settings, or downloaded files back to us. The app works locally on your device. If KoalaPull downloads yt-dlp, ffmpeg, or update information from third-party sources, those requests go to the respective third-party service, not to us.

No tracking

We do not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking pixels, tracking cookies, external CDNs, externally hosted fonts, or third-party scripts on this website.

Legal basis

The legal basis for the short technical processing of request data is our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR: delivering a secure and functional website. No consent banner is needed because we do not set non-essential cookies or use tracking.

Recipients and retention

We do not sell visitor data, share it for advertising, or transfer it to analytics providers. Technical request data is processed only for the time needed to deliver the website. We do not keep website logs.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, data portability, and complaint to a supervisory authority. Because we do not keep visitor profiles or logs, we usually cannot identify a visitor after a request has ended.
